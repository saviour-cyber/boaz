About BDMIS

The Belgut Dairy Management Information System is designed to modernize dairy farming practices in the Belgut region. By integrating technology into daily operations, BDMIS enhances data accuracy, streamlines processes, and improves decision-making for farmers.

Our system provides mobile accessibility, allowing farmers to access critical information anytime, anywhere, ensuring they remain informed and responsive to their operations.
